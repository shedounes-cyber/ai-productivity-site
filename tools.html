Recommended AI Tools

Explore the AI‑powered apps we use to save time and earn more money by automating tasks, generating content and managing workflows.

Zapier

Zapier is an automation platform that connects your favorite apps and services. Its AI features — including Copilot, AI by Zapier, Agents and Chatbots — help you orchestrate tasks across hundreds of integrations. Use Zapier to build workflows that run while you sleep and free up hours for billable work.

Try Zapier

ChatGPT

ChatGPT by OpenAI assists with drafting emails, coding and research. The latest version (GPT‑5.2) is powerful enough to act as a virtual assistant, and the free tier offers capped access that’s perfect for hobbyists and small businesses.

Use ChatGPT

Claude

Anthropic’s Claude excels at summarizing long documents and generating creative content. It respects user safety and can handle larger inputs than many other chatbots. The free plan provides generous usage limits.

Use Claude

Google Gemini

Google’s Gemini is a suite of AI models for writing, coding and data analysis. The free version integrates with Google Workspace tools like Docs and Sheets, making it easy to automate reports and brainstorming.

Try Gemini
